VetBridge AI

Helping veterans translate military experience into civilian career language.

VetBridge AI is a simple career support tool that helps veterans turn military duties, leadership experience, and accomplishments into resume bullets, job search keywords, transferable skills, and interview talking points.

What This Tool Does

Many veterans have valuable experience, but civilian employers may not always understand military job titles, duties, or accomplishments. This tool helps translate that experience into clear, professional language that can be used for resumes, cover letters, job searches, and interviews.

Project Purpose

This project was created to support veterans who may struggle to explain the value of their military experience to civilian employers. It demonstrates AI-assisted problem-solving, user-centered design, career support, resume development, and practical technology for veteran career transitions.
